Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes! We ship to over 50 countries worldwide. Shipping rates and delivery times vary by location. Contact us for a custom shipping quote for larger furniture items.

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day return policy for most items in original condition. Custom or made-to-order pieces are final sale. Please see our full return policy for details.

Do you offer design consultations?

Absolutely! We offer complimentary 30-minute consultations at our showroom, or virtual consultations via video call. Book through our contact form above.

Can I request custom pieces?

Yes, many of our artisan partners accept custom orders. Lead times typically range from 8-12 weeks depending on the piece. Contact us to discuss your vision.

Are your products sustainable?

Sustainability is core to our mission. We use eco-friendly materials, support fair trade practices, and offer a repair program to extend the life of your pieces.
