Walnut Coffee Table

$899

Elevate your living space with our signature Walnut Coffee Table. Hand-finished by skilled artisans, this stunning piece features a solid American black walnut top with natural edge details that showcase the wood's unique character.

The minimalist brass legs provide a warm contrast to the rich walnut tones, creating a perfect balance of modern design and timeless craftsmanship. Each table is one-of-a-kind, with natural variations in grain pattern and color that make it truly yours.

Specifications

  • Dimensions 48" W × 24" D × 18" H
  • Material Solid American Black Walnut
  • Legs Brass with antique finish
  • Finish Hand-rubbed natural oil
  • Weight 45 lbs
  • Assembly Legs require attachment

  • Maintenance Re-oil annually with furniture oil
